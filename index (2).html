Common Questions

Before You Sign Up

Everything we get asked before someone joins — and a few things we wish more people asked.

Do I need to be home for visits?
No. Most of our members aren't. You can provide a lockbox code, leave a door unlocked the day of, or be home if you prefer — your choice. Access details are stored confidentially in your client file and we'll always give you 24–48 hours advance notice of your visit window.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice — no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Just send us an email or text. We'll wrap up your final visit and return any keys or lockbox codes within 7 business days.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Rock Canyon Property Management LLC carries general liability insurance covering bodily injury and property damage. We're happy to provide a certificate of insurance on request. We're also fully bonded for your peace of mind.
What happens if something breaks during a visit?
If we cause it, we own it — we'll make it right at no cost to you. We document the condition of your home at the first visit so there's a clear baseline. Pre-existing conditions and manufacturer defects aren't our responsibility, but anything that happens on our watch is.
How fast do you respond if I need something urgent?
Members get priority response. For routine add-on requests we typically schedule within the same week. For genuine emergencies — water leak, no heat in winter, security concern at a vacant home — we triage immediately and coordinate the right licensed pro fast.
What's NOT included in my membership?
Major repairs and specialty work — anything requiring a licensed electrician, plumber, or HVAC tech for repair (rather than maintenance). We coordinate those professionals for you with full transparency: their cost plus a 15% coordination fee. Add-on services like gutter cleaning, holiday lights, or dump runs are also separate.
How does billing work?
Your first month is charged on the date of your first scheduled visit — not when you sign up. After that, your monthly fee runs automatically on the 1st of each month using a secure payment processor. Add-ons and supply overages are invoiced separately and only with your prior approval. You're never charged for anything you didn't agree to.
Do you serve my area?
We currently serve Utah County, Salt Lake County, Summit County (including Park City), and surrounding Utah communities and cities. If you're not sure, just call or text 385-376-8354 — we'll let you know straight away.
